Pesarifu works by using data extracted from your MPesa statements to build your report. To use it you need to follow the following steps:

  1. Generate your MPesa statements using one of the available methods
  2. Submit the generated statement, the PDF password if it has one and the email you want your report to be sent to
  3. Open your email and open the e-mail from the Pesarifu Reports sender
  4. Navigate to the generated report
  5. Use the report to inform you decision making!

Pesarifu is an application that provides transparency to you Mobile Money through tailor made analytical reports.

There are two ways to generate the M-Pesa statement for your number:

Via M-PESA App:

  1. Log in to the M-PESA App by entering your M-PESA pin
  2. On the home page, Select M-PESA Statements
  3. Select the Month
  4. Select Export Statements and choose a date to export transactions for
  5. Select Generate Statements and proceeed with step 2 of using Pesarifu

Via *334# menu:

  1. Open the phone or dialer app on your phone and dial 
    *334#
  2. Select My Account (option 7)
  3. Select M-Pesa Statement (option 3) then M-Pesa Statement again in the next menu (Option 1)
  4. Select Request Statement
  5. Select Full Statement
  6. Choose one of the options for the period
  7. Enter an e-mail address to receive the statement
  8. Once you receive the statement and a message with the password, proceed to step 2 of using Pesarifu
